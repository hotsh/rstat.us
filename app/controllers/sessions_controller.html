We have a bit of an interesting feature with the POST to /login. Normally, this would just log you in, but for super ease of use, we’ve decided to make it sign you up if you don’t have an account yet, and log you in if you do. Therefore, we try to fetch your user from the DB, and check if you’re there, which is the first half of the if. The else is your run-of-the-mill login procedure.
